\section{Thực nghiệm và phân tích kết quả}

\subsection{Demo Deep Learning: LoRA Rank Sweep}

Muc tieu cua demo la minh hoa truc tiep thong diep cua tutorial: factorization
co the lam giam so tham so can huan luyen trong khi van giu duoc hieu nang co
ich. Demo su dung LoRA cho bai toan text classification va so sanh nhieu che do
fine-tuning.

\subsubsection{Thiet lap thuc nghiem}

Code duoc dat trong thu muc
\texttt{lab-3/deep-learning}. Demo duoc thiet ke de chay bang \texttt{uv}, moi
cau hinh thuc nghiem nam trong thu muc \texttt{configs/}. Cac thanh phan chinh:

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Dataset}: GLUE SST-2 sentiment classification. Khi khong tai
    duoc dataset, code co fallback toy dataset chi de kiem tra pipeline.
    \item \textbf{Model}: \texttt{prajjwal1/bert-tiny}, mot Transformer nho de
    phu hop voi moi truong tinh toan han che.
    \item \textbf{Baselines}: frozen encoder va full fine-tuning.
    \item \textbf{LoRA configs}: rank $r \in \{2,4,8\}$.
    \item \textbf{Metrics}: validation accuracy, validation loss, trainable
    parameters, trainable ratio va thoi gian chay.
\end{itemize}

Tat ca cac so lieu trong bang ket qua duoc lay truc tiep tu file
\texttt{results/runs/<run\_name>/metrics.json} va file tong hop
\texttt{results/plots/metrics.csv}. Thuc nghiem duoc chay tren GPU Tesla T4
voi seed bang 42, moi cau hinh chay 3 epoch tren subset SST-2 gom 5000 mau
train va 872 mau validation.

Ket qua cho thay full fine-tuning dat validation accuracy cao nhat, 0.7661,
nhung phai cap nhat toan bo 4,386,178 tham so. Trong khi do, LoRA rank 2 dat
0.7385 validation accuracy voi chi 2,306 tham so trainable, tuong duong
0.0525\% tong so tham so. Nhung con so nay minh hoa truc tiep thong diep
parameter-efficient adaptation: LoRA mat khoang 2.75 diem accuracy so voi full
fine-tuning, nhung giam gan nhu toan bo chi phi cap nhat tham so.

Mot diem dang chu y la rank cao hon khong nhat thiet cho ket qua tot hon trong
thiet lap nay. LoRA rank 2 tot hon rank 4 va rank 8 ve best validation accuracy.
Dieu nay co the den tu viec mo hinh nen la \texttt{bert-tiny}, dataset duoc chay
tren subset nho, va moi cau hinh chi huan luyen 3 epoch. Vi vay, ket luan phu
hop khong phai la rank 2 luon tot nhat, ma la rank nho da du de dat trade-off
tot trong thuc nghiem minh hoa nay.

\subsubsection{Nhan xet va han che}

Tu Bang~\ref{tab:lora-results} va Hinh~\ref{fig:lora-accuracy-vs-params}, co
the thay LoRA tao ra diem nam giua frozen baseline va full fine-tuning: chat
luong gan voi full fine-tuning hon frozen baseline, nhung so tham so trainable
gan voi frozen baseline hon. LoRA rank 2 chi dung 2,306 tham so trainable, it
hon full fine-tuning khoang 1902 lan, nhung dat accuracy 0.7385 so voi 0.7661.

Hinh~\ref{fig:lora-trainable-params} nhan manh loi ich ve compression: khi dung
LoRA, so tham so can cap nhat tang theo rank nhung van rat nho so voi full
fine-tuning. Hinh~\ref{fig:lora-loss-curves} cho thay full fine-tuning tiep tuc
giam validation loss sau 3 epoch, trong khi cac cau hinh LoRA co xu huong bao
hoa som hon. Day la dau hieu hop ly vi LoRA bi gioi han capacity boi rank thap.

Han che cua thuc nghiem la model duoc chon nho de phu hop tai nguyen tinh toan,
thoi gian huan luyen chi 3 epoch va tap train chi dung subset 5000 mau. Do do,
ket qua khong nen duoc dien giai nhu mot benchmark day du cho LoRA tren moi tac
vu NLP. Tuy nhien, demo da dap ung muc tieu cua do an: co baseline, co mo rong
rank sweep, co metric dinh luong, co visualization, va minh hoa ro rang trade-off
giua hieu nang va so tham so can cap nhat.
